Product Features:

  • Minor Trimming of Factory Front Bumper Required: For a clean installation, trimming the factory bumper is necessary ensuring a seamless fit and optimal functionality.
  • Manufactured from 3/16” USA Steel: This heavy-duty steel ensures long-lasting durability and can withstand rough terrain and harsh conditions.

  • Top-Mounted Winch Control/Solenoid Boxes Must Be Relocated: For better performance and ease of use, the winch control and solenoid boxes need to be relocated to the side of the winch.
  • Light Mounting Brackets for 30” Curved/Straight Dual Row LED Light Bar: Visibility is crucial when off-roading at night, and this bumper accommodates a 30-inch curved or straight dual-row LED light bar for extra illumination on the trail.
  • Heavy Duty CNC Machined 1” Thick Recovery Points with 1” Bore: The bumper includes heavy-duty recovery points that are CNC machined for precision, allowing you to recover your truck easily when needed.
  • Designed and Manufactured in California: Proudly made in the USA, specifically in Livermore, California, each bumper is crafted to meet the highest standards of quality.
  • Ships Complete with 3-Stage Black Texture Powder Coat: The bumper is shipped fully finished with a durable black powder coat, providing corrosion resistance and a sleek appearance.
  • LED Lamp and Winch Not Included: While the bumper is fully equipped for mounting a winch and LED lamp, both items are sold separately.
